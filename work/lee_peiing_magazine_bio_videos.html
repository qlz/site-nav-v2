人物側寫 / Profile

李培瑛博士
Dr. Pei-Ing Lee

南亞科技總經理暨執行董事
President & Executive Director, Nanya Technology Corporation

南亞科技總經理暨執行董事。李培瑛博士是半導體產業資深高階主管，長年在全球科技產業中擔任領導角色。目前他服務於台灣 DRAM 大廠南亞科技，帶領公司在波動的全球記憶體市場中推動技術升級、產能布局與長期策略。

President & Executive Director, Nanya Technology Corporation. Dr. Pei-Ing Lee is a distinguished semiconductor executive with decades of leadership in the global technology sector. He currently serves at Nanya Technology, a Taiwan-based global DRAM company, guiding technology advancement, capacity planning, and long-term strategy through volatile memory-market cycles.

策略領導

他推動南亞科長期成長與現代化布局，並監督先進製程與新北市新廠等重大擴建計畫，以回應 AI、5G、IoT 與雲端運算帶來的記憶體需求。

He has led long-term growth and modernization initiatives, including advanced-node manufacturing and the New Taipei fab expansion designed for AI, 5G, IoT, and cloud demand.

全球商務

在現職之前，他曾任南亞科技全球業務暨行銷資深副總經理與公司發言人，建立全球商業合作關係與企業信任。

Before his current role, he served as Senior Vice President of Worldwide Sales and Marketing and as company spokesperson, building global commercial partnerships and trust.

專業根基

他擁有美國紐約雪城大學化學工程博士學位，並將深厚技術背景結合高階產業管理經驗；同時參與福懋科技與力成科技等董事會治理。

He holds a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Syracuse University and combines a technical foundation with executive management experience; he also contributes board-level oversight at Formosa Advanced Technologies and Powertech Technology.
